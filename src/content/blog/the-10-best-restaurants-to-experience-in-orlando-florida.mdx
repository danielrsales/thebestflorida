## 1. Victoria & Albert’s
Contemporary Fine Dining Excellence

![](https://thebestflorida.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/victoria-albert-s.jpg)

Located inside Disney’s Grand Floridian Resort, Victoria & Albert’s represents the pinnacle of fine dining in Orlando. The restaurant offers multi-course tasting menus crafted with precision, seasonal ingredients, and refined presentation. Service is formal, meticulous, and deeply personalized, creating an immersive culinary experience rather than a simple dinner reservation.

Each dish is plated with artistic intention, and wine pairings are curated to elevate every course. Guests consistently describe the evening as “once-in-a-lifetime” and “worth every penny,” particularly for special occasions.

## 5. Hunger Street Tacos
Creative Mexican Street Food with Authentic Roots

![](https://thebestflorida.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/hst-winterpark-wide-1024x462.jpg)

Hunger Street Tacos has become one of the most respected casual dining spots in the Orlando area for elevated Mexican street cuisine. Located in Winter Park, the restaurant focuses on handmade tortillas, bold salsas, slow-cooked meats, and fresh toppings that balance authenticity with modern execution. The menu is compact but intentional, allowing each dish to showcase depth of flavor rather than overwhelming variety.

The atmosphere is relaxed, vibrant, and community-driven, making it equally suitable for quick lunches and laid-back dinners. Guests consistently highlight the freshness of ingredients and the noticeable care in preparation. While the setting is casual, the culinary quality rivals many upscale Latin concepts in larger cities.

## 6. Capa
Spanish-Inspired Steakhouse with Rooftop Views

![](https://thebestflorida.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/ORL_1644_original-1024x576.jpg)

Capa, located atop the Four Seasons Resort Orlando, blends Spanish culinary influence with premium steakhouse execution. The menu includes expertly grilled cuts, fresh seafood, and tapas designed for sharing. The wood-fired grill imparts depth and smokiness to the meats, while the small plates reflect refined Mediterranean balance.

What truly distinguishes Capa is its rooftop setting. Guests dine with panoramic views and, on select evenings, views of nearby fireworks. The atmosphere is upscale yet modern, making it a frequent choice for romantic dinners, celebrations, and high-end business gatherings.

## 8. Prato
Modern Italian with Rustic Execution

![](https://thebestflorida.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Prato-Orlando_0b3dd55e-caed-4e01-a6fd-887df73a70a3-1024x576.webp)

Prato has earned its reputation as one of Orlando’s strongest Italian dining destinations. The restaurant focuses on wood-fired pizzas, handmade pastas, and ingredient-driven dishes inspired by traditional Italian cooking. The flavors are clean, simple, and carefully executed, emphasizing balance over excess.

The space itself is lively yet refined, with an open kitchen that adds energy to the dining room. It is particularly popular for date nights and social gatherings. Diners frequently mention the consistency of pasta texture and the depth of flavor in even the simplest dishes.

## 9. Kadence
Intimate Omakase Experience

![](https://thebestflorida.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Kadence_Credit-Michael-Mitra-SoReal-Unlimited-Inc._SeptemberFinalWeb-15-1024x576.webp)

Kadence is one of the most exclusive dining experiences in Orlando, offering an intimate omakase format with very limited seating. Guests sit directly at the counter as the chef prepares each piece of sushi with precision and focus. The experience is personal, quiet, and deeply culinary.

The fish selection is premium and often imported, with attention paid to temperature, knife work, and rice seasoning. Because of its format, reservations are essential and typically booked in advance. It is ideal for serious sushi enthusiasts seeking authenticity and craftsmanship.

## 10. The Polite Pig
Modern Barbecue with Elevated Sides

![](https://thebestflorida.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/polite-pig-capa-1-1024x768.jpg)

The Polite Pig brings a contemporary twist to traditional barbecue. Located at Disney Springs, it offers house-smoked meats, inventive sides, and a strong craft beer and bourbon selection. The brisket, pulled pork, and ribs are consistently praised for tenderness and balanced smokiness.

While it operates in a fast-casual format, the food quality surpasses expectations typically associated with that service style. The environment is energetic and welcoming, making it a reliable option for families and casual gatherings.
