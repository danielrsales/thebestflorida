Orlando is one of the most visited destinations in the United States, welcoming millions of travelers every year who come to experience world-famous theme parks like Walt Disney World Resort and Universal Orlando Resort. While ride-sharing services exist, renting a car is still one of the most convenient ways to explore the city and its surrounding attractions. Having your own vehicle gives you the freedom to visit shopping outlets, restaurants, beaches, and nearby cities on your own schedule.

To help travelers choose the best option, we selected ten car rental companies in Orlando that consistently receive strong customer feedback and reliable ratings. These businesses are known for their vehicle availability, convenient locations, competitive pricing, and customer service. Whether you’re looking for a budget car for a quick trip or a luxury vehicle for a special vacation, these rental companies offer dependable options.
## **1. Enterprise Rent‑A‑Car**
![](https://thebestflorida.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/IMG_6756.jpeg)

Enterprise is one of the most recognized car rental companies in the United States and a very popular choice for visitors arriving in Orlando. The company offers a wide range of vehicles including compact cars, SUVs, luxury cars, and family-friendly minivans. Many travelers appreciate the straightforward booking process and the availability of vehicles at multiple locations across the city.

Another reason Enterprise stands out is its focus on customer service and transparent pricing. Many customers highlight the cleanliness of the vehicles and the professionalism of the staff, making it a reliable option for tourists who want a smooth pickup process at the airport or nearby rental locations. The company is widely chosen by families visiting Orlando’s attractions due to its large vehicle selection and flexible rental terms.
