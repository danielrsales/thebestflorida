The city has engineered a distinctive category: resort-driven pool clubs that blend beach aesthetics, immersive design, elevated food & beverage programs, and structured access control.

This guide evaluates the most notable pool club experiences in the Orlando area based on:

## 1) Stormalong Bay
**Located at Disney’s Yacht & Beach Club Resort**

![](https://thebestflorida.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/pools-beach-club-resort-00-1024x576.webp)

Stormalong Bay remains the most architecturally ambitious pool complex in Orlando. Spanning three acres, it integrates a sand-bottom lagoon, shipwreck waterslide, lazy river, and multiple hot tubs into a cohesive maritime environment.

The sand-entry feature is what differentiates it structurally. Guests experience genuine beach texture underfoot, something extremely rare in resort design. Capacity limits preserve experience quality, though peak seasons require early arrival.

The adults-only infinity pool elevates exclusivity. Meanwhile, the family area and lazy river are separated enough to preserve tranquility.

## 7) Hyatt Regency Grotto Pool
Located at Hyatt Regency Orlando

![](https://thebestflorida.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/MCORO-P0392-Grotto-Pool-Aerial.16x9-1024x576.webp)

The Grotto Pool complex at Hyatt Regency Orlando delivers a strong balance between convention-hotel efficiency and resort-style leisure. Built around a landscaped grotto with waterfalls and a waterslide tower, it creates a visually layered aquatic space without overwhelming scale.

Unlike Disney or Universal properties, the crowd here is more diversified — conference attendees, business travelers extending their stay, and leisure guests. The energy is moderate, making it suitable for both families and adults seeking structured relaxation.

## 8) Waldorf Astoria Pool
Located at Waldorf Astoria Orlando
