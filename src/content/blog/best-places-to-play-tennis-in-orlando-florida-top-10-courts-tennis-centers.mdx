## 1. USTA National Campus
![](https://thebestflorida.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Captura-de-tela-2026-02-10-091956-1024x463.png)
The USTA National Campus is widely considered one of the most impressive tennis facilities in the United States. With more than 100 courts and multiple playing surfaces, it serves players of all levels, from beginners to elite athletes. Reviews frequently highlight the organization, cleanliness, and the professional structure of the facility.

Players often mention the diversity of programs available, including leagues, clinics, junior development, and national tournaments. While it can feel large and busy, most agree that the quality and scale of the campus make it a unique tennis destination.
