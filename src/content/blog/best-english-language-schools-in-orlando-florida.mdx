## Final Notes — Choosing the Right English Program
When selecting an English language school in Orlando, consider:

✔ Your primary objective — academic preparation, conversation fluency, or exam focus✔ Class format — intensive, semi-intensive, part-time, or full-time✔ Learning environment — small class sizes versus larger community settings✔ Visa eligibility if you’re an international student

Each institution above has something unique to offer. The best choice depends on your personal goals, schedule, and preferred learning style
